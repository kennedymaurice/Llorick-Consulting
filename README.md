# Llorick Consulting

## Advancing Global Health Through Evidence, Strategy, and Impact

Llorick Consulting is a global health, research, policy, and development advisory firm committed to helping institutions design evidence-based solutions for stronger health systems, healthier communities, and sustainable development outcomes.

The organization supports governments, development partners, NGOs, research institutions, universities, healthcare organizations, foundations, and private-sector actors through strategic advisory, research, monitoring and evaluation, capacity strengthening, policy support, and institutional development.

---

## Website Overview

The Llorick Consulting website serves as a digital platform showcasing the firm's expertise, services, thought leadership, partnerships, and impact across global health and development.

The platform is designed to position Llorick Consulting as a premium international advisory organization operating at the intersection of:

- Global Health
- Public Health
- Research and Evidence Generation
- Health Systems Strengthening
- Monitoring, Evaluation and Learning (MEL)
- Policy and Governance
- Capacity Building
- Climate, Nutrition and Sustainable Development
- Strategic Communication and Knowledge Management

---
